How to Vote

How simple is it to vote using our DDS software?

1

Click the VOTE NOW! button

The big black button at the right of the taskbar, should be hard to miss ;-)

2

Verify your details

Enter your first name, last name and email along with any other required information. This is needed to know who voted.

3

Vote

After verifying your details, then you can choose and vote for who you decide on.
